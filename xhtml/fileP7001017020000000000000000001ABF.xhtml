Potential GDP per Person

Just as real GDP per person is a better measure of living standards than is real GDP, potential GDP per person is a better measure of maximum living standards than is potential GDP. Potential GDP per person equals potential GDP divided by the population. Potential GDP per person is the highest material standard of living the economy is normally capable of producing if all existing inputs are fully employed.
