Seasonal Unemployment

Jobs like fruit picking or snow shovelling are only available at certain times of the year. Seasonal unemployment arises because of seasonal changes in the weather and is a healthy part of an economy that adapts to the seasons. It is a function of nature. Those of us who do not live on the west coast might wish for a more California-like climate, but other than wishing, nothing else needs to be done. Seasonal unemployment is not a problem needing a policy solution.
