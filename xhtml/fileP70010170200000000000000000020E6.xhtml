The CPI Shopping Basket

Figure 7.7 shows the proportion of each category of spending in the CPI shopping basket. A category’s proportion in the basket reflects its proportion in a typical consumer’s budget. For example, housing is about 27 percent of the basket, reflecting that 27 percent of an urban consumer’s spending is on housing (renting or owning). Transportation is 20 percent of spending; food is 16 percent; recreation, education and reading are 11 percent. The smallest category, at about 3 percent, is for alcoholic drinks and tobacco products.
