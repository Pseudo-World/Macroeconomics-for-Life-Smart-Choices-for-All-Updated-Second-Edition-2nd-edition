Calculating the CPI

The CPI calculates average prices for each of the 600 products and services and weights them by the category for each. That weighted average of prices is the Consumer Price Index.

To make it easier to compare years, prices are measured against a base year. The base year is currently 2002, and the cost of the basket for that year is set at 100. The CPI for 2018 takes the same basket of products and services but priced at 2018 prices, giving a value of 133.90. The same products and services that cost $100 in 2002 cost $133.40 in 2018.
