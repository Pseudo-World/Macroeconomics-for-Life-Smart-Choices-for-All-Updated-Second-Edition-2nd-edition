Aggregate Demand Summary

You might feel overwhelmed by the choices behind aggregate supply and aggregate demand, and the lists of factors that change or “shock” aggregate supply and aggregate demand. But as we move on, I will help you absorb this information, which will be amazingly helpful for thinking about the ­fundamental macroeconomic question. The model of aggregate supply and aggregate demand will help you understand business cycles and government policy options — hands-off or hands-on — for responding to them.
