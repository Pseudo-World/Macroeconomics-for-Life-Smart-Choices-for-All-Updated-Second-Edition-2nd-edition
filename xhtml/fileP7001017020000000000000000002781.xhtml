Hit or Miss the Macroeconomic Performance Targets? The Aggregate Supply and Aggregate Demand Model

  1. 8.4 Use the aggregate supply and aggregate demand model to explain the macroeconomic performance of real GDP, unemployment, and inflation.

The model of aggregate supply and aggregate demand enables you to explain real-world macroeconomic events. For example, if there is a recession in the United States, what happens to GDP in Canada, to our unemployment, or to inflation? The aggregate supply and aggregate demand model gives you a framework for analyzing economic news and making smarter choices for your personal success.
