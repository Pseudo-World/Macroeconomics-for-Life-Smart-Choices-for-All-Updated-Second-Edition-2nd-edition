Liquidity

Why would you hold money rather than buy a Ford bond? By holding money you give up the $500 a year in interest.

All reasons for wanting to hold money and give up earned interest are summarized in one word — liquidity. Liquidity is the ease with which assets can be converted into the economy’s medium of exchange. Money — which is by definition the medium of exchange — is the most liquid of all assets. No conversion is required to use money as a means for paying for products and services. Bonds are much less liquid. It may take days to sell your bonds to get your money.
