Money! We all want it. We all work hard for it. But what is it? All the references to “cash in your wallet” sound so old-fashioned. What is money these days and where does it come from?

What’s a Money?

Throughout history there have been only four basic forms of money: commodity money, convertible paper money, fiat money, and deposit money.
