Look Ma, No Reserves!

While the Bank of Canada carefully supervises all banks and financial institutions, you will be surprised to know that chartered banks are not legally required to keep any cash reserves at all! Canadian banks actually hold less than 1 percent of the value of all demand deposits as cash!

Nonetheless, the Canadian banking system is one of the safest and most admired banking systems in the world. There are many reasons for this, but we will focus on just a few related to the basics of deposits and loans. First, most bank customers rarely demand cash. Think about the small proportion of bills or purchases that you pay for with currency.
