Excess Demand for Canadian Dollars

What happens if the exchange rate is US$0.60 per Canadian dollar? The quantity demanded of Canadian dollars is $90 billion. But the quantity supplied of Canadian dollars is only $30 billion. As in any market with a shortage, competition among buyers (demanders) for the scarce Canadian dollars causes the price of a Canadian dollar to rise. As long as the exchange rate is below US$0.90, there is excess demand.
