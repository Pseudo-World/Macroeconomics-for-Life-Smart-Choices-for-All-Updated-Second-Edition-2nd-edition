International Effects of Interest Rates

The international path, from the rest of the world (R.O.W), is highlighted in blue. It also starts with the banking system, where the Bank of Canada sets short-run interest rates. Interest rates affect the value of the Canadian dollar, which is not shown on this diagram. As explained in Chapter 10, changes in the exchange rate affect the prices that the rest of the world pays for Canadian exports (X) and the prices that Canadians pay for imports (IM).
