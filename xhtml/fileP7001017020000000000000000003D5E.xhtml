Deficits Are a Flow

A flow is an amount per unit of time. Your income is a flow. To say your income is $2000 makes no sense, unless we know if it is $2000 a week, $2000 a month, or $2000 a year. The number is meaningful only when there is a time dimension. Deficits (and surpluses) are flows. They must be measured for a specified time dimension.
