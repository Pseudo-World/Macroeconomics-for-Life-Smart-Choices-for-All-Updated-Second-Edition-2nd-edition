What’s So Wonderful about Free Trade? Protectionism and Trade

  1. 13.2 Explain how competition creates winners, losers, and opponents to trade, and analyze three forms of protectionism.

If voluntary trade brings mutual benefits, why do so many people and politicians oppose “free trade”? Arguments to “buy Canadian,” or to “protect Canadian businesses and jobs” by taxing or excluding inexpensive imports are arguments against free trade. Complaints against the “outsourcing of Canadian jobs to non-Canadians,” or calls for government to compensate domestic producers “harmed by foreign competition” are calls for government to take a hands-on role in international trade. Economists usually come down on the side of free trade. What do they see that’s different from the critics of free trade?
