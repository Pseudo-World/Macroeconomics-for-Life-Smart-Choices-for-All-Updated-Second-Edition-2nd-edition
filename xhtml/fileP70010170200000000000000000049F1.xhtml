  9. 9. True. Increase or decrease interest rates.

  10. 10. False. It is politically unpopular higher interest rates decrease GDP and income.

  15. 15. False. Both camps agree that inflation control targets are an effective compromise between hands-off emphasis on rules and hands-on emphasis on government discretion.
